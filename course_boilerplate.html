CSSE2010 - Introduction to Computer Systems

Overall: 7/10

Difficulty: 5/10

Workload: 10-12hr/week

Year Taken: 2024 Sem 2

ECP

Core course for Electrical engineering and Computer Science. Teaches logic gates, combinational circuits, sequential circuits, ALU (Arithmetic Logic Unit), ISA (Instruction Set Architecture), AVR Assembly Introduction, Timers, PWM (Pulse Width Modulation).
You will be assessed on building a circuit and coding a game that interacts with physical systems (e.g. buttons, switches, LED matrix displays).

Very loaded content wise. You do get your own lab kit which is very fun, as electronics starting out tend to be expensive. Very rewarding if you end up putting in the time for it. Must show up to the labs.

Recommended to take an easy course alongside your semester if you want to do well, unless you have prior experience.
